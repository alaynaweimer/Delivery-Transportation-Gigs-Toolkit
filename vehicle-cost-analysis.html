üìö Vehicle Cost Analysis Guide

Your vehicle is your biggest expense. Every mile costs money‚Äîgas, maintenance, depreciation.

Gas Vehicles:

  • Lower upfront cost
  • Higher fuel costs ($0.14/mile @ 25 MPG, $3.50/gal)
  • More maintenance (oil changes, exhaust, transmission)
  • Best for: Drivers with low miles or existing paid-off vehicle

Hybrids:

  • Medium upfront cost
  • Lower fuel costs (~50 MPG = $0.07/mile)
  • Less maintenance than gas
  • Best for: High-mileage drivers (300+ miles/week)

Electric Vehicles (EVs):

  • Higher upfront cost
  • Lowest fuel costs (~$0.04/mile electricity)
  • Minimal maintenance (no oil, no transmission)
  • Tax credits available
  • Best for: Max earnings mode, high-mileage drivers

When to Switch Vehicles:

If you're driving 400+ miles/week in a gas guzzler (15 MPG), you're spending $93/week on gas alone. A hybrid would cost $37/week‚Äîsaving $56/week ($2,912/year).
